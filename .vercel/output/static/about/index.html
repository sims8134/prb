About

Who am I?

I have been living in Sofia, Bulgaria for a few years now, and I have been passionate about history since I was a child. From the World War I battlefields in Alsace to the World War II bunkers in the south of France, and now to the abandoned Cold War bases here in Bulgaria — history has always been how I spend my free time.

A big fan of flea markets, I started collecting historical objects at the age of 12. Since moving to Sofia, I decided to dedicate all my free time to the subject of the People's Republic of Bulgaria.

Why this website?

After noticing a lack of information and a kind of "taboo" around the People's Republic of Bulgaria here in Bulgaria, I decided in 2023 to create an Instagram page dedicated to my collection: Instagram Page. This way, people from all over the world could learn more about this short historical period (1944–1990).

Then in 2024, as a full-time enthusiast, I built this website to bring together the history of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, my collection, tourist sites to visit, and souvenirs to buy — all in one place. So anyone interested in this topic doesn't need to search through 10 different websites to find what they're looking for.

History can of course be controversial. In creating this site, I tried to be as neutral as possible. As stated, this website has been created for cultural and historical purposes only — it is in no way the tool of any political ideology. This way, this historical period can be preserved for future generations.

Who is this website for?

This website is for anyone who is deeply interested in history — particularly the Cold War era in the countries of the communist bloc. It is also a useful place for curious visitors who have heard of Bulgaria and would like to visit, or who want to buy a souvenir from the former People's Republic of Bulgaria.
